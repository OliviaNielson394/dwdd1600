Welcome to my Art Portfolio: A Brief Introduction

This art portfolio was created to display some of the works that I have created. I am hoping to connect with potential clients and collaborators. If you are looking for specific art types, the tabs at the top of the screen can guide you to my digital or material designs. For a more comprehensive look at my art, the Collective Works page houses a wide range of styles, separated into different project categories.

Some materials that I work with the most include:

  • Pencil/Graphite
  • Charcoal
  • Acrylic, Oil, and Watercolor paints
  • Ink
  • Chalk Pastels
  • Marker
  • Mixed media: ie Newspaper, Magazine, Foil, Glue, Cardstock, etc

Studies

Studies found inside my Collective Works page, as well as their respective Digital and Material design pages include emphasis on:

Figure Drawing
I have a number of figure drawings, from quick gesture sketches to long form studies. These pieces include works done in a studio setting with live models, in a natural setting with bystanders, and with online references.
Color Studies
Some of the works included were created as color studies. For each piece I focused on the Tints, Tones, and Values in the image or swatch chart.
Iterations
I have completed several iteration projects, the most prominent in this portfolio being a collection of 100+ eye iterations. Iterations are projects where an object or idea is rendered repeatedly with different conceptualizations and styles.
Pattern
Several of these works emphasise pattern. The composition revolves solely around displaying pattern in these works. Often, it is combined with principles of positive and negative space.
